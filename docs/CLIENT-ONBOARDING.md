## B. INFORMATION CHECKLIST

Send this to the client after the kickoff call as a reference. Collect everything within the first 3 days.

### Required (Must Have)

- [ ] **Google Business Profile login** ‚Äî Email and password (we need access to optimize)

- [ ] **10-20 project photos** ‚Äî Before/after shots are gold. High resolution.

- [ ] **Full list of services offered** ‚Äî Every service they do, even small ones
- [ ] **Service areas** ‚Äî Cities, neighborhoods, zip codes they want to target

- [ ] **Preferred contact method for leads** ‚Äî Phone call, form submission, or both?

  - How long have you been doing this?
  - What makes you different?
  - What do you take the most pride in?

- [ ] **Competitor websites they admire** ‚Äî "Any contractor websites you've seen that you like? Doesn't have to be your trade."

### Nice to Have

- [ ] **Branding guidelines or color preferences** ‚Äî If they have brand colors, we use them. If not, we choose based on trade and market.
- [ ] **Video content** ‚Äî Walkthrough of a project, testimonial videos, day-in-the-life footage

- [ ] **Unique selling points** ‚Äî Warranties, guarantees, free estimates, same-day service

**If they need time:**

**If they're dragging (past 3 days):**
```

send those over today? Don't want to push back your launch date."
```

‚Üí Frame it as their launch date being at risk, not you nagging.
