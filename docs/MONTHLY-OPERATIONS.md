## E. REVIEW GENERATION SYSTEM

Reviews are the #1 ranking factor for local SEO. This system should generate 2-5 new reviews per month for each client.

### The Process

1. **After each completed job**, the client sends this text to the customer:

```
Hey [customer name], thanks for choosing [company name]!
If you have a minute, a Google review would mean the world
to us: [direct Google review link]
```

2. **If no review after 3 days**, follow up once:

```
Hey [customer name], just following up ‚Äî if you had a good
experience with us, we'd really appreciate a quick Google
review. Here's the link: [direct Google review link]
Thanks again!
```

3. **Do not follow up more than once.** Two messages total, max.

### How to Get the Direct Review Link

1. Go to Google Maps
2. Search for the client's business
3. Click on their listing
4. Click "Write a review"
5. Copy the URL ‚Äî this is their direct review link
6. Use a URL shortener if needed for texting

**Alternative:** Google "Google review link generator" ‚Äî several free tools create the direct link from the Place ID.

### Setting This Up with the Client

On the kickoff call or first monthly call:

```
"One thing that's going to make a huge difference is Google
reviews. Here's what I need from you: after every job, send
the customer a quick text asking for a review. I'll give you
the exact template and the link. It takes 10 seconds. Deal?"
```

**Make it stupid easy for them.** Give them the template pre-written. Give them the link ready to paste. If they have to think about it, they won't do it.

### Review Response Protocol

**5-star reviews:**
Respond within 24 hours. Personalize it.
```
"Thank you so much, [Name]! It was great working on your
[project type]. We appreciate your trust and the kind words.
Don't hesitate to reach out if you need anything!"
```

**4-star reviews:**
Respond warmly. Don't address the missing star.
```
"Thanks for the review, [Name]! Glad we could help with your
[project]. We always strive for the best, and your feedback
helps us keep improving. Thanks again!"
```

**1-3 star reviews:**
Respond professionally. Take it offline immediately.
```
"Hi [Name], we're sorry to hear about your experience. We take
every piece of feedback seriously and want to make this right.
Could you give us a call at [phone]? We'd love the chance to
address your concerns directly."
```

**Never:**
- Argue with a negative review
- Get defensive
- Share details about the job publicly
- Ask someone to change their review in the public response

## F. QUARTERLY STRATEGY REVIEW (15 Minutes)

Every 3 months, replace the standard monthly call with a strategy review.

### Agenda

**1. Performance Recap (5 min)**
- Summarize the last 3 months of metrics
- Highlight trends (traffic up, leads steady, rankings improving)
- Celebrate wins ("You went from page 3 to page 1 for [keyword]")

**2. What's Working, What Isn't (5 min)**
- Which content is driving the most traffic/leads?
- Which service pages convert best?
- Any lead quality issues?
- Are they closing the leads they're getting?

**3. Strategy for Next Quarter (5 min)**
- Adjust content focus based on performance
- Discuss any business changes:
  - New services they want to promote
  - New areas they want to target
  - Seasonal shifts in demand
- Set goals for next quarter (leads, rankings, reviews)
